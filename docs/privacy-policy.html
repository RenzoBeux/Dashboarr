Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026

Dashboarr ("the App") is a free, open-source mobile application that lets you manage your self-hosted media services (such as qBittorrent, Radarr, Sonarr, Overseerr, Tautulli, Prowlarr, and Plex) from a single interface.

Data Collection

Dashboarr does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. The App has:

  • No user accounts or authentication system
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No third-party data sharing
  • No cloud services or remote backend

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Service URLs and API keys — the connection details you enter for your self-hosted services. API keys are stored using your device's secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android).
  • App preferences — your configuration settings such as enabled services and display preferences.

This data never leaves your device. It is used solely to communicate directly with your own self-hosted services on your local network or via your own remote access setup.

Network Permissions

The App requires network access to communicate with your self-hosted services. All network requests go directly from your device to your own servers — no data is routed through any intermediary.

Location Permission

The App may request location permission solely to detect your current Wi-Fi network name (SSID). This is used to automatically switch between local and remote service URLs depending on whether you are on your home network. Your location data is never stored, transmitted, or shared — it is used only for this on-device network detection.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external communication is with the self-hosted services you configure yourself.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the GitHub repository.
